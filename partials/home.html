I am a front-end web developer with a background in architecture and graphic design. I believe the highest use of technology is to help people live better, and good design is a critical but often undervalued component of living well. Good design in technology combines well-written code with the right tools for the project (and not just what's hot and trendy) to create the best experience for the user. With that in mind, my work attempts to make beautiful things that work well for people. I welcome all feedback, thoughts, and opportunities to collaborate.
